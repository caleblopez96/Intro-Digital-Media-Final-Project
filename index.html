Mission Statement

World Zoo strives to protect, promote, and advance efforts that support the maintenance and repair world-wide bio-diversity.

We belive that we need to strive to stop the diminishing of population of all wild life species.

We work closely with other zoological and botanical societies to fund conservation projects to create self-sustaining animal and plant populations as well as the global culture required to maintaining over the long term.

We Strive to Make a Difference

We provide resources to animals that are currently in danger of extinction

By investing and participating in research, we place endangered species in safe and healthy environments to ensure the path of recovery.

We approach these goals with a collaborative approach towards any organization (public or private) involved in specific initiative to protect the genetic diversity of our planet.
